# Online Retail Business Analysis with Asana Backlog

## Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end business and data analysis case study based on online retail transaction data. The goal is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and business opportunities.

The project also demonstrates project management and backlog planning using an Asana-style workflow.

## Business Context

A UK-based online retail company wants to better understand its sales performance and customer behavior. The company needs insights that can support decisions related to marketing, product focus, customer retention, and international sales growth.

## Business Problem

The business wants to identify:

- which products generate the most revenue,
- which countries are the most valuable markets,
- which customers are the most valuable,
- whether sales show seasonal patterns,
- what actions can improve sales and customer retention.

## Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

1. Define business questions and KPIs.
2. Prepare and clean transactional retail data.
3. Analyze revenue, products, countries, and customers.
4. Perform customer segmentation using RFM analysis.
5. Create a business dashboard.
6. Prepare actionable business recommendations.
7. Document the project backlog and workflow.
